About me

Welcome to My Personal Website!!
I am a self-driven, quick starter, and passionate researcher with a curious mind, thriving on solving complex and challenging real-world problems. My professional journey revolves around the fascinating realms of nanotechnology, pharmaceutical science, chemical engineering, analytical chemistry, organic chemistry, and biotechnology.

Currently, I am deeply immersed in the synthesis of porphyrinoid-based nanomaterials. My focus revolves around crafting self-assembled nanoparticles, covalent organic frameworks, and metal-organic frameworks. The overarching goal of my work is to harness these materials for their potential applications in antibacterial photodynamic activity and photocatalysis. In essence, I am actively involved in creating nanomaterials with unique porphyrin structures that demonstrate efficacy in combating bacteria through light-based processes, while also exhibiting remarkable photocatalytic properties. This research holds significant promise for advancing materials that can find applications in the realms of medicine, particularly in the domain of antibacterial treatments, and environmental science, with potential implications in photocatalytic processes.

Driven by a relentless curiosity, I find joy in exploring the intersections of various scientific disciplines, pushing the boundaries of what is possible in the pursuit of knowledge and excellence. My work is not just a profession; it's a passion that fuels my commitment to making meaningful contributions to the advancement of healthcare and the betterment of human lives.

As you navigate through my website, I invite you to delve into the projects, research endeavors, and experiences that have shaped my professional journey. Thank you for visiting, and I look forward to connecting with fellow enthusiasts, collaborators, and those who share a similar zeal for innovation and scientific exploration.
